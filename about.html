My Story

I'm a User Experience Designer in Washington, DC. I have a BA in Psychology from the University of Virginia but somehow ended up in tech. My interest in human behavior has led me from Gilmer Hall to the world of product design and the pursuit of helping people reach their goals with less friction. I spend my free time reading Haruki Murakami, podcasting about whether the book was better than the movie, supporting Chelsea FC, and taking Roxy to the dog park.
